- `\d` **Numeric characters, ==[0-9]**

    asdasdasd1942sdasd

- `\w` **Word characters including letters, numbers, underscore**

    9823__sdsd

- `\s` **[\n ]**

    9823__sdsd

- `\D` **Non-numeric**

- `\S` **Non-space**

- `\W` **Non-word**

- `.` **Any characters excluding \n**

- `^` **Beginning of line**

    `^a`

- `$` **End of line**

    `d$`
